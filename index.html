📚 Temperature Scales

🌡️ Celsius (°C)

Celsius is the most commonly used temperature scale worldwide. Water freezes at 0°C and boils at 100°C.

🔥 Fahrenheit (°F)

Fahrenheit is primarily used in the United States. Water freezes at 32°F and boils at 212°F.

❄️ Kelvin (K)

Kelvin is the SI base unit of temperature used in science. Absolute zero is 0 Kelvin.
